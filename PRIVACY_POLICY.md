# Privacy Policy for StedNet-Lock

This privacy policy describes how StedNet-Lock handles your information.

## Information Collection and Use

StedNet-Lock is built to be private and secure. 

* **No Data Collection**: We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data, usage statistics, or device information.
* **Local Operations**: All actions performed by the app, including network switching and profile configuration, occur entirely on your local device.
* **No Network Transmissions**: The app does not connect to any servers, nor does it send any data over the internet.

## Device Permissions

StedNet-Lock requests specific permissions to function correctly:

* **Shizuku Service Access**: This is used solely to access the system APIs necessary to toggle network modes and change profiles. No personal or sensitive data is accessed, read, or modified by this service.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party tracking, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this file within the repository.

## Contact

For questions or suggestions regarding this privacy policy, please open an issue in this repository.
